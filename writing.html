This World Still Knows Nothing (WIP) – novel, adult, space opera
A university student studying astrophysics, Jeonghwa has always wanted to make discoveries on the frontier, where researchers study the unknown. When she discovers her research mentor murdered, she starts to wonder what was so dangerous about his own existence. Jeonghwa soon meets Sol, another professor at her school who seems to know more about the ordeal than they reveal. They tell her that her mentor knew something he shouldn't have—and that an organization tried to keep him quiet. But what did he know?

Jeonghwa desperately wants to find out, even if it puts her in danger. She and Sol begin a journey to find the people who killed him and why. Along with them is Kadir, a trained pilot and son of an empress. Motivated by duty and the mundanity of his own life, he escapes his home planet in search of something that might help his family and people. Together, the three jump through wormholes, visiting planets across the colonized universe in order to reach the mysterious frontier—where there is said to be a discovery so dangerous it must be kept secret at all costs.

Sunsets of a Universe (2021) – short story collection, young adult, science fiction
goodreads | kindle | paperback
Sunsets of a Universe is a science fiction short story collection featuring young adults involved in the creation and application of the science and engineering of the future. These are stories of hope and resilience, and the fight to make the world around us a better place.

Stories include “The Sound of Salt,” “The Ship at the Edge of the World,” and “Genesis.”

The Dark Mage (2020) – novella, young adult, fantasy
goodreads
When Hwa-Young is attacked, along with her pirate crew, she begins the hunt for those who tried to hurt her. Along the way, she encounters a childhood friend and they team up, realizing they're both after the same thing. Through it all, something else begins to make its way back into Hwa-Young's life—her magic.
