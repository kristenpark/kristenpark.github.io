Books

Sunsets of a Universe – short story collection, young adult, sci-fi (November 2021)

Goodreads

Sunsets of a Universe is a science fiction short story collection featuring young adults involved in the creation and application of the science and engineering of the future. These are stories of hope and resilience, and the fight to make the world around us a better place.
Stories include “The Sound of Salt,” “The Ship at the Edge of the World,” and “Genesis.”

The Dark Mage – novella, young adult, fantasy (May 2020)

Goodreads

When Hwa-Young is attacked, along with her pirate crew, she begins the hunt for those who tried to hurt her. Along the way, she encounters a childhood friend and they team up, realizing they're both after the same thing. Through it all, something else begins to make its way back into Hwa-Young's life—her magic.
