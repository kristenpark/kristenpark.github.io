about

Kristen Park is a Korean-Chinese American student, poet, and novelist from New Jersey. She enjoys writing science fiction, fantasy, and contemporary fiction. Her work explores identity, human connection, and the impacts of science.

In her free time, Kristen can be found reading young adult novels, listening to K-Pop and film scores, playing Genshin Impact, and journaling. In the future, she hopes to work as an engineer with a focus in medicine and biology. She will be an incoming freshman at Johns Hopkins University.
