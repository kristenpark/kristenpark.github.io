about

Kristen Park (she/her) is a Korean-Chinese American writer from New Jersey. She writes science fiction, fantasy, and contemporary fiction, exploring themes of identity, human connection, and the future of science and engineering. Her poetry has been recognized by the National YoungArts Foundation and the Scholastic Art & Writing Awards.

In her free time, she can be found reading poetry, listening to music, doing crosswords, and drinking iced tea. She also enjoys fashion and photography, occasionally at the same time. Currently, she is a first year at Johns Hopkins University studying applied mathematics and statistics with a minor in writing seminars.
