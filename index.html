about

Kristen Park (she/her) is a Korean-Chinese American student, poet, and novelist from New Jersey. She enjoys writing science fiction, fantasy, and contemporary fiction, exploring themes of identity, human connection, and the impacts of science on society and individuals. Her poetry has been recognized by the National YoungArts Foundation and the Scholastic Art & Writing Awards.

In her free time, she can be found reading young adult novels, listening to K-Pop and film scores, watching movies, and making journal spreads. In the future, she aspires to work as an engineer with a focus in medicine and biology. Currently taking a gap year, she will be a freshman at Johns Hopkins University in fall 2023.
