about

Kristen Park (she/her) is a Korean-Chinese American writer, student, and researcher. Raised in Brooklyn, New York and the suburbs of New Jersey, she currently spends most of her time in Baltimore, Maryland. She writes science fiction, fantasy, and contemporary fiction, exploring themes of identity, human connection, and the future of science and engineering. Through poetry, she examines cultural traditions, mental health, and the relationship between time and identity. Her writing has been recognized by the National YoungArts Foundation and the Scholastic Art & Writing Awards.

In her free time, she can be found reading poetry, listening to music, doing crosswords, and drinking matcha lattes. She also enjoys fashion and photography, and daydreams about traveling the world. Currently, she is a third year undergraduate at Johns Hopkins University studying Applied Mathematics and Statistics with a minor in Writing Seminars.
