about

Kristen Park (she/her) is a Korean-Chinese American student, poet, and novelist from New Jersey. She enjoys writing science fiction, fantasy, and contemporary fiction, exploring themes of identity, human connection, and the future of science and engineering. Her poetry has been recognized by the National YoungArts Foundation and the Scholastic Art & Writing Awards.

In her free time, she can be found reading poetry, listening to rap and film scores, watching movies, and drinking iced tea. She will be a freshman at Johns Hopkins University in fall 2023 and plans to study applied mathematics and statistics.
