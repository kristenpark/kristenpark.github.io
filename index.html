about

Kristen Park is a Korean-Chinese American student, poet, novelist, and researcher from New Jersey. She enjoys writing young adult science fiction, fantasy, and contemporary. Her work explores identity, the impacts of societal expectations, and human connection.

She also has a deep interest in STEM and enjoys doing independent research that relates biology and artificial intelligence. She hopes to pursue a career in biomedical engineering in the future.

In her free time, she can be found reading young adult novels, listening to K-pop, and journaling.
