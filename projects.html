research

Undergraduate Research in the Trayanova Lab (April 2024 - Present)

Develop and evaluate personalized 3D digital heart models for tetralogy of Fallot patients based on LGE-MRI data. Place landmark points in CardioViz software and run MATLAB analysis program to generate regions of densely scarred and healthy myocardium. Generate methods and objectives for project on statistical shape analysis of patient hearts with tetralogy of Fallot.

Analysis of the Health of Brassica oleracea var. sabellica Leaves in an Aeroponics System Using Machine Learning (September 2019 - March 2020)

Constructed an indoor aeroponics system to grow kale from seeds. Designed and optimized a convolutional neural network to classify healthy and unhealthy kale leaves with 98.1% accuracy.

Predicting the Onset of Heart Disease Using Machine Learning (April 2019 - May 2019)

Developed a logistic regression machine learning model that used numerical health data to predict whether patients had heart disease with 84.2% accuracy.
